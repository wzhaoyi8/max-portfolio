Overview

In the heart of Addis Ababa, informal settlements struggle under the weight of mounting waste. This project addresses the challenge by turning discarded materials into opportunities for architectural innovation — repurposing waste to design adaptable furniture, reimagine living spaces, and breathe new life into communities.

From small-scale interventions within homes to large-scale transformations of public streets, the approach promotes sustainability and strengthens social bonds. By celebrating collaboration and creativity, the project reshapes urban landscapes one upcycled space at a time.
