Overview

At RoboticPlus.AI, a Shanghai-based computational fabrication consultancy, the internship focused on the parametric design and performance optimization of kinetic architectural systems. The "Lotus" is one of three flagship projects — a large-scale kinetic facade in Changsha, China — alongside the West Bund Center and TOD Subway Station.

The work involved analyzing kinetic mechanisms for the moving facade panels, creating precision 3D-printed physical models for client presentations, and running performance simulations to optimize structural behavior. A key outcome was reducing GRC mold material waste by 85% — from 2,000㎡ to 300㎡ — through computational geometry optimization. The internship also included designing and building the company website roboticplus.tech.
